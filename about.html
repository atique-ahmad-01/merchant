Our Story

Furniture That Feels Like Home

We believe that beautiful, functional furniture should be accessible to everyone. That's the idea behind Uni Merchant Store.

Who We Are

Born from a Love of Great Design

Uni Merchant Store was founded with a simple belief: that you shouldn't have to compromise on style to stay within budget. We source contemporary, well-crafted furniture and bring it directly to your door.

Our collection focuses on the heart of the home — the living room. From elegant oval coffee tables to slim minimalistic side tables, every piece is selected to complement the way people actually live today.

We partner with Homme Concept to bring you furniture that is designed in the Scandinavian tradition — clean lines, natural textures and enduring simplicity — all at an honest price.
